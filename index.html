THE WAKE-UP CALL

Europe stands at a crossroads. While America undergoes its cultural revolution and China consolidates its digital hegemony, we face a critical question: Who controls the data, controls reality.

The Paradox

  • 500 million inhabitants
  • GDP comparable to the United States
  • Equivalent technological spending

We have the means. We have the market. We have the history. But we don't have the System.

We're told we lack innovation. False. The gap is structural, not technological. We import 80% of our digital technologies. We finance our own dependency, creating a critical systemic vulnerability.

The era of innocence is over. To bridge this gap, we must forge our own tools.

THE MISSION: RECODING THE ECONOMY

CircularOS (Circular Operating System) is not an application. It is the sovereign, distributed operating system that allows Europe to circulate its resources, data, and values freely without external dependence.

Why an "OS" for the Economy?

In the 21st century: Whoever controls the Data controls Reality.

Today, the European economy runs on foreign operating systems:

  • Our commercial data is hosted on clouds subject to extraterritorial laws
  • Our logistical flows depend on opaque algorithms
  • Our social interactions pass through extractive platforms

We digitized our economy but forgot to build the foundations. We are tenants on our own continent.
